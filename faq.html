Frequently Asked Questions

🟣 Data365 Help Center

Need help? Find answers to the most common questions about Tasks, Lucky Codes, Withdrawals, Referrals and more.

💜 How do I earn on Data365? +
You earn on Data365 by completing simple tasks, referring your friends and family, and redeeming Lucky Codes.
🎁 How does Lucky Code work? +
Lucky Codes are posted every Wednesday and Saturday in the official Data365 WhatsApp Channel. Copy the code, paste it into the Redeem Code page, and claim the attached bonus before it expires.
📷 Why do I need to upload a screenshot? +
Your screenshot serves as proof that you genuinely completed the task. Without a valid screenshot, your task cannot be approved.
❌ Why was my task declined? +
Your task may be declined because the task wasn't completed properly, the screenshot was unclear, you submitted the wrong screenshot, or you unfollowed or left the required platform after completing the task.
👥 How does the referral program work? +
Share your unique referral link or code. When your friend signs up, verifies their account, and joins the official Data365 WhatsApp Channel, you'll receive a 40MB referral reward.
⏳ When will I receive my reward? +
Your reward is credited immediately after your submitted task proof has been reviewed and approved by the admin.
📤 How do I withdraw data? +
Withdrawals are available from 500MB up to 700MB. Simply enter your network (MTN, Airtel, Glo or 9mobile), your phone number, and the amount you'd like to withdraw.
⏰ How long does withdrawal take? +
Withdrawals are usually approved within 3–6 hours. During busy periods, they may take a little longer.
📋 How long does task approval take? +
Task approvals usually take between 2–6 hours after submission.
📢 How can I advertise my social media on Data365? +
Visit the Upload Task page, choose the social media service you want (followers, likes, views, members or retweets), select the campaign duration, then message the admin to make payment. Your task will be published after payment is confirmed.
🔐 Is my account safe? +
Yes. Your account is completely safe as long as you follow the platform rules and keep your login details private.
🚫 Are multiple accounts allowed? +
No. Multiple accounts are strictly prohibited. If detected, all related accounts will be permanently banned.
🚫 Why was my account banned? +
Accounts may be banned for creating multiple accounts, submitting fake screenshots, abusing referrals, fraudulent activities, or violating Data365 policies.
💰 Is Data365 free? +
Yes! Data365 is completely free to use. You never have to pay to earn data.
