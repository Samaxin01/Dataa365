Data365 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information such as your email address, username, profile details, task submissions, account balance, and transaction history to provide and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage your account, process withdrawals, verify completed tasks, provide customer support, send notifications, and improve the overall Data365 experience.

3. Data Security

We use secure technologies and Firebase services to protect your personal information. Although we work to keep your data safe, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

4. Task Verification

Screenshots submitted for task verification are used only for reviewing task completion. Fraudulent or edited screenshots may result in rejection or account suspension.

5. Withdrawals

Withdrawal requests are reviewed by our administrators before approval. Any suspicious or fraudulent activity may lead to cancellation of the withdrawal request.

6. Third-Party Services

Data365 may use trusted third-party services such as Firebase and FormSubmit to securely manage authentication, database storage, and task submissions.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of Data365 after updates means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our Customer Support through the Customer Service page in the app.
